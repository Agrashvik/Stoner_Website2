What is Stone Paper?

Stone paper is made from calcium carbonate bonded with a non-toxic resin. It is waterproof, durable, and a more sustainable alternative to traditional paper.

Benefits of Stone Paper

  • Waterproof and tear-resistant
  • No trees are cut down to produce it
  • Uses less energy and water during production
  • Can be recycled to make more stone paper

How It's Made

Stone paper is produced by grinding down calcium carbonate rocks into a fine powder. This is then mixed with a small amount of non-toxic resin to create the paper.
